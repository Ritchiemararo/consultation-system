About Us !

The Nairobi hospital

The Nairobi Hospital is a non-profit organization that is leading in medical expertise and service provision, with deservedly earned recognition throughout East Africa and beyond as an advanced diagnostic, treatment and referral centre. Investment in the latest technology and medical equipment has seen us establish leadership in medical procedures both in Kenya and beyond. 

We take pride in our highly qualified professionals who deliver our mission everyday, translating their knowledge and expertise to internationally compliant practices in healthcare provision. Courtesy, consideration and unreserved respect towards our patients’ privacy, dignity and confidentiality have helped us earn their trust and goodwill time and again. Our dedicated nursing staff provides professional care within a friendly and comfortable environment, ensuring a pleasant and anxiety-free time in hospital for our patients and their families.
